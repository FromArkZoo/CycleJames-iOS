CycleJames

Indoor cycling workouts that talk to your trainer.

A focused, single-purpose iOS app for structured power-based training on FTMS-compatible smart trainers.

What it does

  • Built-in workout library covering VO2max, threshold, sweet spot, endurance, and recovery sessions.
  • Live target-power control over Bluetooth (FTMS) — your trainer sets resistance automatically.
  • Heart-rate display from any standard BLE HRM.
  • Adjust intensity mid-workout, add intervals on the fly, or tweak whole-workout power before you start.
  • Local ride history with TSS, IF, and normalized power. No account, no cloud, no tracking.
