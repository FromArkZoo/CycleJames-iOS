Support

Common questions

My trainer won't connect.

CycleJames supports trainers that speak the FTMS Bluetooth profile (most modern smart trainers — Wahoo Kickr, Tacx Neo, Wattbike Atom, Saris H3, Elite Direto). Make sure no other app is currently paired with the trainer (Zwift, the trainer's vendor app, etc.) — Bluetooth allows only one connection at a time. Power-cycle the trainer if needed.

Where do I set my FTP?

Settings tab → "Current FTP". Workouts use this number as the basis for target watts.

The target power doesn't match what I expected.

Workouts are written as percentages of FTP. If your FTP setting is wrong, every target will be off proportionally. Use the Overall Power +/- buttons on the workout detail screen to scale before you start, or +/- during the ride to adjust the active interval in 5W steps.

Does my ride save if my phone locks during the workout?

Yes. The ride clock runs from wall-clock time and Bluetooth keeps streaming in the background, so locking the phone mid-workout doesn't lose data. Stop the ride from inside the app to write the session to history.

How do I delete my data?

Uninstall the app. CycleJames keeps no copies anywhere else.
