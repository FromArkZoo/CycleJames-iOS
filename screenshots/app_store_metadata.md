### Description (≤4000 chars)

> CycleJames is a focused, single-purpose indoor cycling app for structured
> power-based training on FTMS-compatible smart trainers.
>
> Connect your trainer over Bluetooth. Pick a workout. The app writes target
> watts to the trainer in real time so resistance follows the plan
> automatically — no head unit required.
>
> WHAT YOU GET
> • Built-in workout library covering VO2max, threshold, sweet spot, endurance,
>   and recovery sessions, from 30 minutes to 2 hours.
> • Live target power streaming over Bluetooth (FTMS protocol) — your trainer
>   sets resistance for you.
> • Heart-rate display from any standard Bluetooth heart-rate monitor.
> • Adjust intensity mid-ride: fine-tune the active interval in 5W steps, or
>   insert a custom interval on the fly when you want to push harder.
> • Workout builder — design your own steady or ramp intervals and save them.
> • Local ride history with average power, normalized power, intensity factor,
>   and TSS for every session.
> • Calendar view of your training week and month.
>
> PRIVACY
> CycleJames keeps everything on your device. No accounts. No cloud. No
> analytics. No tracking. Uninstall the app and your data goes with it.
>
> COMPATIBLE TRAINERS
> Any smart trainer that supports the standard FTMS Bluetooth profile, which
> includes most modern trainers from Wahoo, Tacx, Wattbike, Saris, Elite,
> JetBlack, and others.
>
> REQUIREMENTS
> iPhone running iOS 17 or later, plus an FTMS-compatible Bluetooth smart
> trainer. A Bluetooth heart-rate strap is optional.
